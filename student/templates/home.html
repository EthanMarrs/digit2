Quality maths education is critical to the future success of any country. In resource-poor environments, mobile technology presents us with an opportunity to make sure people have access to the education they deserve.

Dig-it is a gamified mobile maths challenge platform for High School learners. Learners earn points, badges and airtime by solving maths challenges with an emphasis on content from all sections of the syllabus. Using Human-Centred Design processes our team developed an e-learning platform which recognised the needs, incentives and behaviours of our target users.
